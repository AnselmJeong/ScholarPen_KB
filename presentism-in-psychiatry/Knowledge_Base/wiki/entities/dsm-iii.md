# DSM-III

## What It Is
DSM-III, published in 1980, is the third edition of the *Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders*, a symptom-based diagnostic manual that replaced psychoanalytic theory with explicit operational criteria. Its shift to observable symptoms reshaped psychiatry, becoming the foundation for biological research and modern classification while later showing limited alignment with genetic and neurobiological findings.
