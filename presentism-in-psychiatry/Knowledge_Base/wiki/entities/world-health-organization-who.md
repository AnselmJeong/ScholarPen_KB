# World Health Organization (WHO)

## What It Is
The World Health Organization is a specialized agency of the United Nations responsible for international public health. It establishes global health standards and collects data to track disease and disability. In this literature, it is highlighted as a primary authority for diagnostic classifications and global health statistics.

## Role in the Literature
* In [[001-1-puzzles-of-mental-illness]]: Provider of global disability data related to mental illness.
* In [[009-9-the-successes-and-failures-of-the-dsm-revolution]]: Promoted the global "epidemic" of depression as a leading cause of disability.
* In [[From Madness to Mental Health - 15]]: International body producing the ICD standards.
