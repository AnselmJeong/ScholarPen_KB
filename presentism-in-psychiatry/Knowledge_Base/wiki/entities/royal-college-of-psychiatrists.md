# Royal College of Psychiatrists

## What It Is
The Royal College of Psychiatrists is the professional body for psychiatrists in the United Kingdom. In this literature, it serves as the institutional framework for debating the ethics of psychiatric practice and the historical legacy of the profession.

## Role in the Literature
* In [[2022 - Lepping, Poole - Psychiatry's need for Vergangenheitsbewältigung; ‘culture wars’, cognitive dissonance and coming to terms with the past]]: Cited as a professional body making initial steps to acknowledge racism while still requiring systemic change.
* In [[2023 - Hilton - Our values and our historical understanding of psychiatrists]]: The professional body analyzing its own institutional history and the ethics of judging past psychiatrists.
* In [[2023 - Hilton - Sailing between Scylla and Charybdis; invited response to ‘Blame or discovery’]]: The institutional home of the author and the publisher of the commentary.
* In [[2023 - Ikkos - Blame or discovery Walter Benjamin's iJetztzeit,i Purdue Pharma LP and ‘our values and our historical understanding of psychiatrists’]]: The professional body whose bulletin published the commentary.
* In [[2024 - Arya - Racism in psychiatry and the case of presentism]]: The professional body and publisher overseeing the journal.
