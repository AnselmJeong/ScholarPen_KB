# Humoral Theory

## Why It Matters
* Provides the foundational physiological framework for early medical understandings of mental health.
* Acts as a historical precursor to more modern [[nosology]] and [[biomedical-model]] approaches.
* Essential for understanding the transition from ancient medicine to early [[alienism]].
