# Neurasthenia

## Why It Matters
* Represents a critical transition point in the [[medicalization]] of mental distress.
* Illustrates the shift from [[moral-treatment]] to a [[biomedical-model]] focusing on organic brain disease.
* Highlights the tension between organic pathology and "without cause" diagnostic criteria.

## Key Positions
| Position | Core claim | Key sources |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Biomedical Transition | Shift from utopian moral treatment to reducing mental illness to organic brain disease. | [[003-3-a-biological-century]] |
| Diagnostic Ambiguity | Reliance on common-sense "without cause" criteria despite the organic paradigm. | [[003-3-a-biological-century]] |
