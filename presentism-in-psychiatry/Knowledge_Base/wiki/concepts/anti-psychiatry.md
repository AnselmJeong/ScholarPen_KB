# Anti-psychiatry

## Why It Matters
* Provides a critical lens for analyzing the legitimacy and power structures of psychiatric practice.
* Acts as a counter-narrative to the established [[biomedical-model]] and psychiatric classification systems.
* Influences the discourse on patient autonomy and the ethics of involuntary commitment.
