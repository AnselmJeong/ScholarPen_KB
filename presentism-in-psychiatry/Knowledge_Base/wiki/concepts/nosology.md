# Nosology

## Why It Matters
* Provides the framework for how mental disorders are categorized and defined over time.
* Highlights the tension between descriptive clinical observation and theoretical biological models.
* Essential for tracing the evolution of diagnostic criteria from early "alienism" to modern systems.

## Key Positions
| Position | Core claim | Key sources |
