# DSM-III

## Why It Matters
* Marks a pivotal shift toward standardized, descriptive diagnostic criteria.
* Represents the formalization of the [[biomedical-model]] within psychiatric practice.
* Serves as a focal point for debates regarding the transition from dynamic to diagnostic psychiatry.
