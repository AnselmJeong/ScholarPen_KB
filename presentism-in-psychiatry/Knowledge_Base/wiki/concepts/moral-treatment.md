# Moral Treatment

## Why It Matters
* Represents a utopian era of psychiatric care focused on psychological and social environments.
* Serves as the historical precursor to the [[biomedical-model]].
* Illustrates the paradigm shift from humanitarian behavioral management to organic brain disease theories.
