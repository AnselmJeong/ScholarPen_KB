# Hysteria

## Why It Matters
* Serves as a critical case study for the evolution of [[nosology]] and psychiatric classification.
* Illustrates the intersection of gender, somatic symptoms, and mental health diagnosis.
* Tracks the transition from early clinical observations to more formalized medical models.
