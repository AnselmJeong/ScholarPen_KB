# Psychiatric classification

## Why It Matters
* Traces the evolution of how mental disorders are defined and categorized over time.
* Highlights the tension between descriptive diagnosis and theoretical etiology.
* Analyzes the impact of standardized manuals (like the DSM) on clinical practice and medical legitimacy.
