# Biomedical model

## Why It Matters
* Represents the shift toward treating mental illness as a physiological disease of the brain.
* Central to the transition from dynamic psychiatry to diagnostic, classification-based psychiatry.
* Forms the theoretical basis for the standardization of psychiatric practice and the use of the DSM.
