# Historiography

## Why It Matters
* Examines the methodologies used to construct the history of psychiatry and psychology.
* Critiques the tendency to project contemporary values onto past medical practices.
* Analyzes how institutional narratives (e.g., APA presidential addresses) shape professional identity.
