# Eugenics

## Why It Matters
- Highlights the historical intersection of psychiatry and state-mandated biological control.
- Critical for understanding the ethical failures and the need for *Vergangenheitsbewältigung* (struggle to overcome the past) within the profession.
- Connects psychiatric classification to systemic human rights abuses.
