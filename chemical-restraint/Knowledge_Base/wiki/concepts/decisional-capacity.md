# decisional capacity

## Why It Matters
- Central to ethical assessments of consent and coercion in psychiatric treatment
- Determines legitimacy of interventions like chemical restraint and involuntary medication
- Subject to ongoing evaluation due to fluctuating mental states and treatment effects
- Intersects with legal capacity, autonomy, and rights-based frameworks in mental health law
