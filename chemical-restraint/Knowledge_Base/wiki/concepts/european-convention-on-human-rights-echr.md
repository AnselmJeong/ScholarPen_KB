# European Convention on Human Rights (ECHR)

## Why It Matters
- Establishes binding human rights standards applicable to psychiatric care and treatment
- Provides legal framework for challenging coercive practices in mental health settings
- Reinforces principles of dignity, autonomy, and prohibition of inhuman treatment
- Intersects with ethical and legal debates on involuntary treatment and restraint
