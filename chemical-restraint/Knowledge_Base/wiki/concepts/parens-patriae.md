# Parens Patriae
## Why It Matters
- Relevant to involuntary psychiatric treatment and civil commitment processes  
- Connects to ethical tensions between state authority and individual autonomy in mental health care  
- Appears in discussions of medical ethics specific to psychiatry's unique challenges  
- Underlies legal and policy debates on decision-making capacity and substituted judgment
