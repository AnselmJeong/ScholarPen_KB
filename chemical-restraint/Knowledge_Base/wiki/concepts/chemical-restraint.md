# Chemical restraint
## Why It Matters
- Central to understanding coercive practices in mental health and aged care settings  
- Highlights ethical tensions between safety, autonomy, and human rights  
- Involves pharmacological interventions with significant clinical and legal implications  
- Subject of growing scrutiny due to inconsistent definitions and measurement across jurisdictions
