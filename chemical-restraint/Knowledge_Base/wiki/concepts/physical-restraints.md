# Physical restraints
## Why It Matters
- Addresses a critical evidence gap regarding benefits and harms of restraints in hospital care  
- Examines comparative intrusiveness of physical versus chemical restraints  
- Relevant to ethical debates on patient autonomy and coercion in healthcare  
- Informing clinical protocols and policy decisions on restrictive interventions
