# violence risk assessment
## Why It Matters
- Critical for preventing harm in clinical settings through early risk identification
- Integrates legal obligations like Tarasoff duty with clinical assessment practices
- Requires structured tools to balance static/dynamic factors and environmental context
- High diagnostic accuracy tools (e.g., AUC 0.88) improve predictive validity over unstructured methods
