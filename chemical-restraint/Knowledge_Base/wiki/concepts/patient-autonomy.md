# patient autonomy

## Why It Matters
- Central to ethical psychiatric practice and human rights frameworks
- Undermined by coercive measures such as chemical restraints and forced medication
- Shapes legal and clinical approaches to consent, capacity, and treatment decisions
- Influences recovery-oriented and person-centered models of care
