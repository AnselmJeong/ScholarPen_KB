# service user involvement
## Why It Matters
- Ensures mental health policies reflect lived experiences, not just procedural metrics  
- Enhances ethical validity of coercive practices by incorporating patient perspectives  
- Supports cross-jurisdictional legal comparisons through standardized experiential data  
- Challenges assumptions about uniform negativity of coercive interventions
