# biomedical model

## Why It Matters
- Central to debates on mental health care ethics and human rights compliance  
- Framed as both scientifically valid and potentially coercive in global discourse  
- Central to tensions between biomedical psychiatry and rights-based reform movements  
- Underpins legal and clinical practices around consent, coercion, and capacity
