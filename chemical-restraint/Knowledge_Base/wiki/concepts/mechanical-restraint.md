# Mechanical restraint

## Why It Matters
- Central to understanding coercive practices in mental health care  
- A focus of international human rights efforts to reduce restraint use  
- Lack of consistent definitions hinders global measurement and comparison  
- Intersects with legal, ethical, and clinical debates on patient autonomy and safety
