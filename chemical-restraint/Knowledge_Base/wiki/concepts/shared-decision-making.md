# shared decision-making

## Why It Matters
- Reduces coercion by centering patient autonomy and dignity in mental health care  
- Supports therapeutic relationships and treatment adherence through collaborative processes  
- Addresses systemic distrust by improving patient participation in care decisions  
- Aligns with human rights frameworks and recovery-oriented care models
