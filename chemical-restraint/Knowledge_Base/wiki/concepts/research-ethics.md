# research ethics
## Why It Matters
- Ensures participant rights and voluntariness in psychiatric research  
- Addresses ethical challenges in clinical trials involving vulnerable populations  
- Guides responsible conduct of research in mental health settings  
- Prevents exploitation and upholds scientific integrity in studies  

## Key Positions
| Position | Core claim | Key sources |
|---------|------------|-------------|
| Ethical research requires valid informed consent | Voluntariness depends on external, intentional, illegitimate, and causally effective influences, not mere pressure | [[Coercive Treatment in Psychiatry - 22]] |
| Global ethical standards guide psychiatric research | The WPA Code of Ethics for Psychiatry provides a living framework for research, education, and clinical practice globally | [[2021 - Appelbaum, Tyano - The WPA Code of Ethics for Psychiatry]] |
