# Six Core Strategies

## Why It Matters
- Reduces reliance on coercion and upholds human rights in mental health care  
- Promotes evidence-based alternatives to restrictive practices  
- Enhances safety through relational and environmental approaches  
- Supports dignity and mutual well-being of service users and staff
