# Paternalism

## Why It Matters
- Central to ethical debates in psychiatric care regarding patient autonomy  
- Drives policy and legal discussions on coercive measures and involuntary treatment  
- Underpins tensions between beneficence and respect for persons in clinical practice  
- Highlighted in recent scholarship as a relational, not merely hierarchical, ethical issue
