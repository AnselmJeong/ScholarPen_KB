# restrictive care practices

## Why It Matters
- High prevalence rates across mental health settings indicate widespread use  
- Extreme heterogeneity in reported rates challenges comparability and policy generalization  
- Inconsistent definitions undermine validity of research and clinical guidelines  
- Raises significant ethical and human rights concerns regarding patient autonomy and dignity
