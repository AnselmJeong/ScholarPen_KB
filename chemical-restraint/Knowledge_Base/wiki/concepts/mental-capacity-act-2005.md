# Mental Capacity Act 2005
## Why It Matters
- Central to legal frameworks assessing decision-making capacity in healthcare
- Balances patient autonomy with protection for those lacking capacity
- Directly informs capacity assessment protocols in clinical settings
- Underpins ethical debates on paternalism vs. autonomy in psychiatry
