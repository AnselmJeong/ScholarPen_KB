# therapeutic relationship
## Why It Matters
- Central to reducing coercion and violence in psychiatric settings by fostering trust and de-escalation
- Directly impacted by stakeholder perspectives on safety, rights, and dignity in care environments
- Shapes patient experiences of coercive measures, influencing whether they are perceived as harmful or beneficial
- Mediates ethical and legal challenges around consent, autonomy, and involuntary treatment
