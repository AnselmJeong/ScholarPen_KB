# psychiatric reform

## Why It Matters
- Addresses systemic issues of coercion in psychiatric care  
- Highlights conflicts between human rights frameworks and clinical practice  
- Reveals gaps in legal and social support systems for vulnerable populations  
- Prompts reevaluation of psychiatric authority and patient autonomy
