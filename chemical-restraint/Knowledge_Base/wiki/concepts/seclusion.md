# Seclusion

## Why It Matters
- Central to discussions on reducing coercive practices in mental health care
- Often studied alongside mechanical and chemical restraint as a restrictive intervention
- Reflects tensions between safety imperatives and human rights in psychiatric settings
- Highlighted in experimental and international efforts to reform coercive care
