# patient experience

## Why It Matters
- Central to understanding the human impact of psychiatric interventions, particularly those involving coercion
- Reveals tensions between clinical objectives and patient autonomy in mental health care
- Highlights how interpersonal dynamics and systemic factors shape subjective experiences of care
- Essential for evaluating the ethical acceptability and effectiveness of treatment approaches
