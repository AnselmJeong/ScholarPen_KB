# haloperidol
## Why It Matters
- Key antipsychotic used in rapid tranquilization for acute agitation
- Frequently studied in emergency psychiatry contexts for managing disturbed behavior
- Part of broader discussions on chemical restraint and involuntary treatment
- Involved in safety debates regarding extrapyramidal side effects and QT prolongation
