# patient-centered care

## Why It Matters
- Centers the patient's perspective in clinical decision-making and care planning
- Challenges paternalistic approaches in psychiatric care
- Promotes reduction of coercive practices through collaborative strategies
- Aligns with ethical imperatives to uphold dignity and autonomy

## Key Positions
| Position | Core claim | Key sources |
|----------|------------|-------------|
| Patient-centered care reduces coercion | Effective psychiatric care minimizes seclusion and restraint by prioritizing patient voice and preventive strategies | [[2020 - Masters et al. - The Role of the Psychiatrist in Seclusion and Restraint]] |
