# CRPD
## Why It Matters
- Central to global human rights frameworks for persons with disabilities, including mental health contexts
- Underpins debates on legal capacity and decision-making in psychiatric care
- Informs international efforts to reduce coercion and promote supported decision-making
- Highlights gaps between policy commitments and real-world implementation in mental health services
