# Randomized controlled trial (RCT)

## Why It Matters
- Central method for evaluating interventions in psychiatric and emergency care contexts  
- Primary evidence source for clinical guidelines on managing acute agitation  
- Enables comparison of pharmacological treatments like benzodiazepines under controlled conditions  
- Supports evidence-based practice in chemical restraint and rapid tranquilization
