# principlism
## Why It Matters
- Central framework in bioethics for ethical decision-making in healthcare contexts
- Frequently applied in psychiatric and medical ethics debates within the archive
- Subject of critique for inadequacy in resolving complex ethical dilemmas
- Influences policies and practices around autonomy, consent, and restraint

## Key Positions
| Position | Core claim | Key sources |
|---------|------------|-------------|
| Critique of principlism | Overemphasizes autonomy, lacks conflict resolution theory, ignores collective impact | [[2024 - Mangal, Scheiner - The Limitations of Principlism]] |
| Critique of principlism | Superficial starting point, lacks theoretical depth, inconsistent in complex cases | [[2024 - Trimble - Ethics; A matter of principle]] |
