# Norwegian Mental Health Care Act
## Why It Matters
- Central legal framework governing involuntary mental health treatment and civil commitment in Norway
- Directly shapes patient experiences with coercive measures in community-based services
- Influences public attitudes toward involuntary admission and treatment policies
- Intersects with international human rights standards on mental health and disability rights
