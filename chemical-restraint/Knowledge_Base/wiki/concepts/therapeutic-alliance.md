# therapeutic alliance
## Why It Matters
- Therapeutic alliance is foundational to effective psychiatric care despite prevalence of coercion
- It represents the relational core where trust, empathy, and mutual respect counterbalance coercive dynamics
- Strengthening alliance improves treatment adherence, reduces perceived coercion, and upholds patient autonomy
- Ethical and legal frameworks increasingly recognize alliance as essential to rights-based, recovery-oriented care
