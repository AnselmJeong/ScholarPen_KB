# psychiatric advance directives

## Why It Matters
- Advances patient autonomy in mental health care by enabling prior consent for treatment
- Reduces coercive interventions by aligning crisis responses with previously expressed preferences
- Supported as a tool to uphold human rights and legal standards like the CRPD
- Challenges remain in implementation due to skepticism about feasibility and safety concerns
