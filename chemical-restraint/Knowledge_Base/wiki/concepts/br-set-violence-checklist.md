# Brøset Violence Checklist

## Why It Matters
- Validated tool for predicting imminent aggression in mental health settings
- Demonstrates strong predictive validity for 24-hour aggression risk
- Supports clinical decision-making for violence prevention strategies
- Comparable performance to Dynamic Appraisal of Situational Aggression (DASA)
