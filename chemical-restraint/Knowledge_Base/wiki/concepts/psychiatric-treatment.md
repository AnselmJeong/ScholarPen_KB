# psychiatric treatment

## Why It Matters
- Central to understanding clinical practice and legal liability in psychiatry  
- Directly informs risk management through adherence to professional standards  
- Intersects with ethics, law, and patient rights in clinical decision-making  
- Foundational for evaluating coercion, consent, and treatment efficacy
