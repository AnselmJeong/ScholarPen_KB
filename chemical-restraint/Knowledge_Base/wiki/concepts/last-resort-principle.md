# last resort principle

## Why It Matters
- Determines legal and ethical boundaries for coercive interventions in psychiatric care  
- Serves as a safeguard against abuse of power in involuntary treatment settings  
- Central to compliance with human rights frameworks and clinical accreditation standards  
- Balances patient safety with preservation of autonomy and dignity

| Position | Core claim | Key sources |
