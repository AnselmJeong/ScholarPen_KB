# Autonomy
## Why It Matters
- Central to ethical debates on coercive interventions in psychiatry  
- Challenges the justification of legally mandated treatment for mental illness  
- Underpins patient rights and dignity in mental healthcare  
- Drives reform efforts toward less restrictive alternatives

## Open questions
- How can autonomy be preserved when individuals lack decision-making capacity due to acute psychosis?  
- What safeguards ensure that legally mandated treatments respect autonomy without compromising safety?  
- Is it possible to balance beneficence and autonomy in psychiatric care without resorting to coercion?  
- How do cultural and social contexts influence perceptions of autonomy in mental health treatment?
