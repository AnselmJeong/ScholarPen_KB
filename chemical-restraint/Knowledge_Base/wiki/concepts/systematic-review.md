# systematic review

## Why It Matters
- Provides a rigorous method for synthesizing evidence across studies to identify patterns and inconsistencies in restrictive care practices
- Highlights methodological challenges in comparing prevalence rates due to inconsistent definitions and measurement approaches
- Supports evidence-based policy and clinical decision-making in mental health and aged care settings
- Reveals gaps in standardization that affect the validity of cross-study comparisons and reporting
