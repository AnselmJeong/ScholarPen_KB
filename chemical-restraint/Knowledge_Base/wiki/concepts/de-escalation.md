# De-escalation
## Why It Matters
- Reduces aggression and physical restraint use in acute psychiatric settings
- Addresses high rates of healthcare workplace violence in psychiatric environments
- Mitigates dual-perspective measurement challenges in coercion research
- Supports healing environment principles through non-coercive intervention
