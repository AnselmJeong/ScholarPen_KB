# chlorpromazine
## Why It Matters
- Central antipsychotic in early rapid tranquilization protocols
- Historically significant in the development of antipsychotic medications
- Relevant to comparative effectiveness and safety assessments of IV agents
- Illustrates transition from oral/IM to IV delivery in acute disturbance management
