# UN-CRPD

## Why It Matters
- Central framework for disability rights and legal capacity in mental health contexts  
- Shapes ethical and legal debates around involuntary treatment and coercion  
- Influences policies on supported decision-making and alternatives to guardianship  
- Drives interdisciplinary dialogue between psychiatry, law, and human rights

|---------|------------|-------------|
