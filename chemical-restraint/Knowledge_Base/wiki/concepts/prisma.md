# PRISMA
## Why It Matters
- Central to evidence-based medicine in psychiatric interventions
- Provides standardized framework for systematic reviews in mental health research
- Ensures transparency and reproducibility in synthesizing evidence on coercive practices
- Supports informed clinical decision-making for acute disturbance management
