# Medical Ethics

## Why It Matters
- Ethics in medicine addresses core issues of autonomy, beneficence, and justice in patient care  
- Psychiatry presents unique ethical challenges due to patient vulnerability and stigma  
- Coercive measures raise significant ethical concerns regarding consent and human rights  
- Ethical frameworks guide clinical decisions in high-stakes mental health interventions
