1. # Physical restraint
2. ## Why It Matters
   - Physical restraint is a coercive measure used in psychiatric settings to manage acute agitation
   - Its use raises significant ethical and human rights concerns regarding bodily autonomy and dignity
   - Reduction in physical restraint use is linked to effective de-escalation strategies
   - Monitoring and minimizing restraint aligns with recovery-oriented and least restrictive care principles
