# heterogeneity
## Why It Matters
- Reveals inconsistencies in measuring and reporting restrictive practices across settings  
- Highlights how geography and methodology influence prevalence estimates  
- Challenges assumptions about uniformity in care practices globally  
- Underscores need for standardized measurement in research and policy
