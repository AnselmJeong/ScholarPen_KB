# UNCRPD

## Why It Matters
- Central international human rights framework guiding mental health law and policy globally
- Promotes rights-based approaches challenging traditional paternalistic models in psychiatric care
- Highlights tensions between legal capacity principles and practical mental health vulnerabilities
- Drives reform efforts to reduce coercion and uphold dignity in mental health services

## Key Positions
| Position | Core claim | Key sources |
|----------|------------|-------------|
| Rights-based alignment | Mental health laws should align with UNCRPD principles of autonomy and dignity | [[2019 - Raveesh et al. - How right is right-based mental health law]] |
