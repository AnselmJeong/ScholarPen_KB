# seclusion and restraint

## Why It Matters
- Highlights ethical and racial disparities in psychiatric care practices  
- Reflects systemic challenges in balancing patient safety with autonomy and dignity  
- Central to ongoing debates about coercion, human rights, and recovery-oriented care  
- Involves legal, clinical, and ethical dimensions requiring interdisciplinary attention
