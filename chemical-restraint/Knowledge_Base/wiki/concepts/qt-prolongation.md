# QT prolongation
## Why It Matters
- QT prolongation is a critical cardiac risk associated with certain psychiatric medications
- It increases risk of torsades de pointes, a potentially fatal arrhythmia
- Relevant in acute agitation treatment where antipsychotics like haloperidol are commonly used
- Monitoring is essential when using chemical restraints in emergency psychiatric settings
