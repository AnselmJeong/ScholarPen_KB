# epistemic injustice

## Why It Matters
- Central to ethical critiques of psychiatric coercion and forced treatment  
- Reveals how systemic biases undermine authentic consent and lived experience  
- Connects to human rights frameworks like the UN CRPD and legal capacity rights  
- Highlights the gap between ethical ideals and clinical practices in mental health care
