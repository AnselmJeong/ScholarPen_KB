# evidence-based practice

## Why It Matters
- Central to evaluating clinical interventions using empirical evidence  
- Drives improvements in patient safety and care quality in mental health services  
- Informs policy and guideline implementation to reduce coercive practices  
- Supports ethical decision-making by integrating best available research  

## Key Positions
| Position | Core claim | Key sources |
|---------|------------|-------------|
| Staff training improves outcomes | Evidence-based staff training significantly reduces physical restraint use in mental health settings | [[2018 - Ye et al. - Staff Training Reduces the Use of Physical Restraint in Mental Health Service, Evidence-based Reflection for China]] |
