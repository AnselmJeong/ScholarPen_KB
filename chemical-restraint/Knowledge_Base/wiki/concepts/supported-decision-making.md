# Supported Decision-Making

## Why It Matters
- Ensures legal capacity and autonomy for persons with disabilities under international human rights law
- Balances respect for personhood with safeguards in complex decision-making contexts
- Challenges substitute decision-making models by prioritizing support over substitution
- Aligns disability rights frameworks with dignity, safety, and indivisible human rights principles
