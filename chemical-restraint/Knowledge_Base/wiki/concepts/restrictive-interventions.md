# restrictive interventions

## Why It Matters
- A significant clinical and ethical challenge in psychiatric and emergency care settings
- Raises critical human rights concerns despite potential necessity for safety
- Highly variable application across European psychiatric inpatient settings
- Frequently used in emergency departments for potentially violent patients, driven by non-clinical factors
