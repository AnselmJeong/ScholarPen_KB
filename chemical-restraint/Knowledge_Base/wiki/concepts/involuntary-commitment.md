# involuntary commitment

## Why It Matters
- Involuntary commitment balances individual liberty with public safety through legal and clinical standards  
- Central to ethical debates on autonomy, coercion, and human rights in mental health care  
- Informs legal reforms and clinical practices regarding psychiatric hospitalization and treatment  
- Intersects with disability rights, due process, and international human rights frameworks

|---------|-----------|-------------|
