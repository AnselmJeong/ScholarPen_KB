# confidentiality
## Why It Matters
- Central to ethical psychiatric care for minors, balancing adolescent autonomy with parental authority  
- Varies significantly across cultural, religious, economic, and political contexts  
- Increasingly challenged by technology use and evolving family dynamics  
- Intersects with legal obligations and ethical dilemmas in treatment decisions
