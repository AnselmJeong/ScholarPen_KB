# early intervention
## Why It Matters
- Central to ethical discourse in psychiatry regarding prevention and human rights
- Intersects with debates on coercion, privacy, and technological innovation in mental health care
- Highlighted as a key area requiring systemic reform and ethical scrutiny
- Relevant to staff perceptions and practical barriers in clinical implementation
