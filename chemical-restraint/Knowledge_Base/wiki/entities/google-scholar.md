# Google Scholar

## What It Is
Google Scholar is a freely accessible web search engine that indexes scholarly literature across academic disciplines, including articles, theses, books, and conference papers. It serves as a broad discovery tool for researchers seeking to supplement formal database searches with grey literature and additional peer-reviewed sources.
