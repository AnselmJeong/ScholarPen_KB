# United Nations Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (UN CRPD)

## What It Is
The United Nations Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (UN CRPD) is an international human rights treaty that establishes comprehensive rights for persons with disabilities, including psychosocial disabilities. It serves as a foundational legal framework promoting equality, non-discrimination, and autonomy in areas such as health, education, and justice. Its significance lies in providing a global standard for protecting the rights of individuals with disabilities, particularly in contexts involving mental health and psychiatric care.
