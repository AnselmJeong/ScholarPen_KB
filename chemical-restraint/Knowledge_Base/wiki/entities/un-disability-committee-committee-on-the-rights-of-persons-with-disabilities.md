# UN Disability Committee (Committee on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities)

## What It Is
The UN Disability Committee, formally the Committee on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities, is the treaty body responsible for monitoring the implementation of the Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (CRPD). It provides authoritative interpretations of the CRPD through general comments and recommendations, shaping international human rights law related to disability.

In [[Mental Capacity, Dignity and the Power of International Human Rights -03]]: the treaty body responsible for interpreting the CRPD through general comments
