# Indian Journal of Psychiatry

## What It Is
The Indian Journal of Psychiatry is a peer-reviewed academic journal in the field of psychiatry, serving as a primary venue for scholarly discourse in Indian mental health. It publishes original research, reviews, and discussions on clinical practice, ethics, and policy, contributing significantly to both national and international psychiatric knowledge.

## Role in the Literature
In [[2016 - Sidhu, Srinivasraghavan - Ethics and medical practice; Why psychiatry is unique]]: The journal served as the peer-reviewed venue for publishing an article on ethics in psychiatric practice.
In [[2019 - Raveesh et al. - How right is right-based mental health law]]: The journal provided the platform for disseminating a review article on mental health law within the Indian psychiatric community.
