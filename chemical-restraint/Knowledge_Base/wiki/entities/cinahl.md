# CINAHL

## What It Is
CINAHL is a comprehensive database of nursing and allied health literature, indexing journals, books, and evidence-based resources in healthcare. It is widely used for systematic and integrative reviews in clinical practice, particularly in mental health and nursing research.

## Role in the Literature
In [[2018 - Cusack et al. - An integrative review exploring the physical and psychological harm inherent in using restraint in mental health inpatient settings]]: One of five databases searched for relevant literature.
In [[2019 - Muir-Cochrane et al. - International research into 22 years of use of chemical restraint An evidence overview]]: Database searched for nursing and allied health literature relevant to restraint practices in clinical settings.
