# International Classification of Diseases (ICD)

## What It Is
The International Classification of Diseases (ICD) is a globally standardized diagnostic tool developed by the World Health Organization for classifying diseases, disorders, and health conditions. It serves as the foundational framework for psychiatric diagnosis and medical coding, underpinning clinical practice, epidemiology, and health policy worldwide.
