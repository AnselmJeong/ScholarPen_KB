# Embase

## What It Is
Embase is a biomedical and pharmacological database that indexes peer-reviewed literature, including journal articles, conferences, and gray literature. It is widely used in systematic reviews for its comprehensive coverage of drug-related and clinical research, making it a critical resource for evidence synthesis in healthcare.

- In [[2024 - Laureano et al. - Ethical Issues in Clinical Decision-Making about Involuntary Psychiatric Treatment A Scoping Review]]: One of five databases searched for relevant literature
- In [[2024 - Parsaei et al. - Machine Learning for prediction of violent behaviors in schizophrenia spectrum disorders a systematic review]]: a biomedical and pharmacological database used in the literature search
