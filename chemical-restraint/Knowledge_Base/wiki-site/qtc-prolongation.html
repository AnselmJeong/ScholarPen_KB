Why It Matters

  • Critical safety concern in acute agitation management due to risk of torsades de pointes
  • Limits medication choices in sequential treatment algorithms for refractory agitation
  • Highlights need for ECG monitoring when using certain psychotropic agents
  • Represents a key adverse event driving risk-informed prescribing in psychiatric emergencies
