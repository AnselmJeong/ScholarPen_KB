Why It Matters

  • Central to debates on mental health law reform balancing patient rights with clinical necessity
  • Highlights tensions between international human rights standards and national legislation
  • Raises ethical concerns about autonomy, dignity, and potential for abuse in psychiatric care
  • Drives development of safeguards and alternatives to involuntary interventions
