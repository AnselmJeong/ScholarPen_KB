Why It Matters

  • Provides synthesized evidence on prevalence and variability of restrictive practices in healthcare settings
  • Highlights methodological challenges in defining and measuring restrictive care across studies
  • Supports evidence-based policy and clinical guidance on restraint and seclusion use
  • Reveals gaps in standardization that affect cross-study comparisons and validity of findings
