Why It Matters

  • Central to ethical tensions in acute psychiatric care involving rapid intervention
  • Highlights challenges in distinguishing treatment from restraint in crisis settings
  • Drives recurring debates over medication use, patient autonomy, and safety
  • Reflects systemic reliance on pharmacological methods amid definitional ambiguity
