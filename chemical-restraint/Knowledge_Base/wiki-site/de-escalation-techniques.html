Why It Matters

  • Critical for minimizing physical and psychological harm from psychiatric restraints
  • Supports patient autonomy and reduces reliance on coercive measures
  • Aligns with ethical, legal, and human rights frameworks in mental healthcare
  • Central to evidence-based strategies for safety and recovery-oriented care
