Why It Matters

  • Guides ethical decisions on coercion by requiring interventions to be proportionate to risks
  • Balances patient autonomy with safety in psychiatric care
  • Ensures least restrictive alternatives are considered before restrictive measures
  • Grounds clinical ethics in structured, transparent decision-making
