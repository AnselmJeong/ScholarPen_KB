Why It Matters

  • Reveals how psychiatric treatments can undermine authentic consent under coercion
  • Highlights systemic biases like sanism that compromise ethical research and care
  • Exposes gaps between legal rights and lived experiences in psychiatric settings
  • Undermines trust in therapeutic relationships when consent is not truly voluntary
