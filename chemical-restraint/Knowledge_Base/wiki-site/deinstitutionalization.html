Why It Matters

  • Central to understanding shifts from institutional to community-based mental health care
  • Directly impacts debates on autonomy, coercion, and human rights in psychiatric treatment
  • Underpins policy shifts affecting civil commitment, involuntary treatment, and community alternatives
  • Highlights tensions between public safety, therapeutic goals, and individual rights
