Why It Matters

  • Central to acute psychiatric care as a frequent presentation requiring rapid intervention
  • Highlights tension between effective symptom control and risks of pharmacological interventions
  • Reveals gaps in clinical guidance for managing treatment-resistant cases
  • Drives development of standardized, safety-focused protocols for emergent medication use
