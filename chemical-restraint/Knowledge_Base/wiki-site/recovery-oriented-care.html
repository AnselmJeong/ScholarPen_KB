Why It Matters

  • Integrates patient autonomy and lived experience into mental health treatment planning
  • Reduces reliance on coercive practices by prioritizing recovery goals and self-determination
  • Aligns with human rights frameworks and ethical standards in psychiatric care
  • Supports implementation of advance directives and alternatives to coercion as tools for empowerment
