Why It Matters

  • Central to ethical debates on psychiatric coercion and human rights frameworks
  • Challenges individualistic models of autonomy by emphasizing relational and social conditions
  • Informs debates on the limits of principlism in psychiatric ethics
  • Integrates civil-political and socio-economic rights under CRPD Article 12
