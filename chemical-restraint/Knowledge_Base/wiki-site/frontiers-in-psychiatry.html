What It Is

Frontiers in Psychiatry is a peer-reviewed, open-access scientific journal that publishes original research, reviews, and clinical studies in the field of psychiatry and mental health. It serves as a key venue for disseminating evidence-based findings relevant to psychiatric disorders, treatment approaches, and neuroscience, contributing to advancements in both academic and clinical practice.
