  1. Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS)

  2. Why It Matters —

    • CMS regulations influence psychiatric restraint and seclusion practices in federally funded facilities
    • CMS oversight impacts compliance with federal standards for patient safety and rights in behavioral health settings
    • CMS policies shape interpretations of restraint use in alignment with federal healthcare quality and equity goals
