Why It Matters

  • Central to understanding the historical and ethical evolution of psychiatric care, particularly regarding coercion and liberty

  • Informs debates on human rights, autonomy, and the legitimacy of involuntary treatment
  • Provides context for analyzing the impact of deinstitutionalization and community-based alternatives
