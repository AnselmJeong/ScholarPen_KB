Why It Matters

  • Central to reducing coercion in mental health settings by promoting patient autonomy
  • Builds therapeutic relationships and improves treatment quality through shared power dynamics
  • Addresses systemic distrust by centering patient voices in decision-making processes
  • Serves as a practical alternative to coercive measures across inpatient and community care
