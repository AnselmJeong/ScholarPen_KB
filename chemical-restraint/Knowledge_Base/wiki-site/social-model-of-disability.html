Why It Matters

  • Challenges medical-model assumptions by framing disability as a social construct
  • Informs disability rights frameworks like the UN CRPD cited in multiple sources
  • Highlights how psychiatric practices may perpetuate social barriers rather than address impairment
  • Provides theoretical basis for supported decision-making and legal capacity reforms
