Why It Matters

  • Central to ethical psychiatric practice and legal protections against coercion
  • Shapes clinical decisions around consent, restraint, and treatment preferences
  • Drives reforms in psychiatric law and advance directive implementation
  • Highlights tensions between safety interventions and patient rights
