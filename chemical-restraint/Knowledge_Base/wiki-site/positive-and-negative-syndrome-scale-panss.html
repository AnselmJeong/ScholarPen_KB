Why It Matters

  • Key tool for assessing symptom severity in schizophrenia and related disorders
  • Used to evaluate treatment response in clinical trials and practice
  • Supports differentiation between positive, negative, and general psychopathology symptoms
  • Informative for understanding psychopathology in agitated or aggressive neuropsychiatric patients
