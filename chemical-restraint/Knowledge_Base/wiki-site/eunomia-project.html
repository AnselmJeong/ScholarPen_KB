Why It Matters

  • Represents a significant initiative addressing coercive practices in psychiatric care
  • Focuses on systemic analysis of coercion beyond individual interventions
  • Contributes to understanding institutional structures shaping patient autonomy
  • Bridges ethical, legal, and clinical perspectives on mental health treatment

Key Positions

Position Core claim Key sources
Systemic view of coercion Coercion in psychiatric care is embedded in institutional structures and social processes, not limited to specific interventions The Use of Coercive Measures in Forensic Psychiatric Care - 04
