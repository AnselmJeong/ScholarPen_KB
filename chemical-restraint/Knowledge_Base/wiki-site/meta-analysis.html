Why It Matters

  • Enables quantitative synthesis of diverse study findings in psychiatric and clinical research
  • Identifies patterns and effect sizes across heterogeneous populations and settings
  • Supports evidence-based decision-making in mental health interventions
  • Reveals sources of variability such as measurement methods and geographic factors
