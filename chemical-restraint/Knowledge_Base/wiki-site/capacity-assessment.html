Why It Matters

  • Central to ethical and legal frameworks governing treatment decisions in psychiatry
  • Balances individual autonomy with protective duties in clinical contexts
  • Serves as a threshold for determining lawful use of coercive or restrictive interventions
  • Informs application of mental health legislation across differing ethical paradigms
