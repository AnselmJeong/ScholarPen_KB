Why It Matters

  • Addresses ethical tension between safety and autonomy in psychiatric care
  • Guides clinical decision-making to minimize harm and preserve dignity
  • Central to human rights frameworks and legal standards in healthcare
  • Informs protocols for restraint reduction and de-escalation strategies
