Why It Matters

  • Central to ethical psychiatric practice and treatment adherence
  • Influences perceptions of coercion in both voluntary and involuntary settings
  • Critical for building trust in therapeutic relationships under legal constraints
  • Moderates patient experience and recovery outcomes in psychiatric care
