Why It Matters

  • Social determinants are essential for understanding mental health beyond biomedical reductionism
  • They reveal how commercialized science obscures structural inequities in research and policy
  • Including social determinants enables meaningful cross-jurisdictional comparisons of mental health laws
  • Addressing them is necessary for rights-based approaches to mental health care
