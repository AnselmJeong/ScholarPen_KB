Why It Matters

  • Central to ethical and human rights debates in mental health care
  • Involves fundamental tensions between autonomy, safety, and treatment efficacy
  • Linked to systemic issues like diagnostic uncertainty and power imbalances
  • Directly impacts patient outcomes, trust in services, and legal compliance
