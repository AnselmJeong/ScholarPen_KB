Why It Matters

  • Central to ethical debates on coercive measures in psychiatry
  • Underpins human rights frameworks like the UN CRPD in mental health contexts
  • Challenges practices that may undermine personhood and autonomy in treatment
  • Drives reform toward less restrictive, dignity-respecting alternatives
