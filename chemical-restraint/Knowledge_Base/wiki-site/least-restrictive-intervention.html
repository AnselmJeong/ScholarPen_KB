Why It Matters

  • Balances patient safety with civil liberty in psychiatric settings
  • Reduces harm from restraint while maintaining clinical effectiveness
  • Guides ethical decision-making in emergency psychiatric care
  • Supports compliance with evidence-based guidelines and legal standards
