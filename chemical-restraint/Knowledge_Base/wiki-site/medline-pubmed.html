What It Is

MEDLINE/PubMed is a major biomedical database maintained by the U.S. National Library of Medicine, indexing peer-reviewed literature in medicine, nursing, dentistry, veterinary medicine, and the preclinical sciences. It is widely used in systematic reviews and evidence-based research to identify relevant clinical studies across global health disciplines.
