What It Is

The European Psychiatric Association (EPA) is a professional organization representing psychiatry across Europe, promoting scientific advancement, clinical excellence, and ethical standards in mental health care. It serves as a coordinating body for national psychiatric associations and advocates for psychiatry’s medical and scientific standing in global discourse.
