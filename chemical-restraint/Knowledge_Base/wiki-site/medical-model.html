Why It Matters

  • Central to understanding the historical development of antipsychotic medications and rapid tranquillisation practices
  • Highlights tensions between biomedical reductionism and social determinants in mental health
  • Represents a dominant paradigm critiqued for prioritizing industry interests over social justice
  • Underpins debates about human rights, coercion, and commercialization in psychiatric care
