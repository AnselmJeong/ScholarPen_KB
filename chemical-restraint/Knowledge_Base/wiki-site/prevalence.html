Why It Matters

  • Quantifies use of restrictive practices across healthcare settings
  • Reveals geographic and methodological variability in prevalence estimates
  • Highlights ethical concerns regarding patient autonomy and safety
  • Informs policy debates on reduction of coercive interventions
