Why It Matters

  • Central to ethical, legal, and clinical debates about patient autonomy vs. public safety in mental healthcare
  • Strong predictor of coercive treatments like forced medication and seclusion
  • Persists despite international human rights frameworks, raising concerns about systemic rights violations
  • Influences therapeutic outcomes and trust in psychiatric systems
