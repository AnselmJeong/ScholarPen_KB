Why It Matters

  • Ensures patient liberty is prioritized in psychiatric treatment settings
  • Provides a legal and ethical framework for minimizing coercion in mental healthcare
  • Supports the development of alternatives to involuntary interventions
  • Balances treatment efficacy with human rights protections in clinical practice

Position Core claim Key sources
