Why It Matters

  • Central to ethical psychiatric care environments
  • Influences use of coercive interventions in inpatient settings
  • Shapes patient safety, dignity, and therapeutic relationships
  • Key focus in training and reform efforts to reduce restrictive practices

Key Positions

Position Core claim Key sources
Supportive of therapeutic milieu as preventive A positive therapeutic milieu reduces need for coercive measures by promoting safety and engagement 2023 - Soto et al. - The Role of Psychiatrists in Seclusion and Restraint Episodes a Curriculum for Psychiatric Trainees
