Why It Matters

  • First global Code of Ethics for psychiatry adopted in 2020 by WPA
  • Influences ethical standards and practices in psychiatry worldwide
  • Serves as reference point for national psychiatric associations’ ethical frameworks
  • Supports harmonization of ethical guidelines across regions, particularly in Europe

Key Positions

Position Core claim Key sources
WPA’s ethical framework First comprehensive Code of Ethics for psychiatry, structured around five adapted medical ethics principles, designed as a living document for global clinical, educational, research, and advocacy use 2021 - Appelbaum, Tyano - The WPA Code of Ethics for Psychiatry
European psychiatric associations’ reliance on international codes National psychiatric associations in Europe primarily rely on international ethical codes rather than developing national ones, with strong support for collaboration under the European Psychiatric Association to build shared ethical guidelines databases 2023 - Samochowiec et al. - Ethical dilemmas in contemporary psychiatry; Findings from a survey of National Psychiatric Associations in Europe
