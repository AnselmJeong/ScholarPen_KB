Why It Matters

  • Central to ethical and legal debates about patient autonomy in psychiatric care
  • Directly impacts human rights compliance and clinical practice guidelines
  • Reflects tension between therapeutic intent and coercive intervention
  • Influences policy development around involuntary treatment and restraint
