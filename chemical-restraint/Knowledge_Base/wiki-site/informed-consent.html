Why It Matters

  • Central to respecting patient autonomy and decision-making capacity in psychiatric care
  • Balances therapeutic intervention with legal and ethical rights to refuse treatment
  • Highlights tension between clinical beneficence and patient self-determination
  • Underpins ethical frameworks for involuntary treatment and coercion debates

Position Core claim Key sources
