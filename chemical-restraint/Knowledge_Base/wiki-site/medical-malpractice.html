Key Positions

Position Core claim Key sources
Liability arises from deviation from standard of care Psychiatric malpractice occurs when treatment fails to meet accepted professional standards, irrespective of intent Clinical Manual of Psychiatry and Law - 05
Ethical alignment supports risk reduction Following sound clinical and ethical practices generally reduces malpractice risk by promoting effective care Clinical Manual of Psychiatry and Law - 05
Reliance on international ethical guidelines National psychiatric associations in Europe favor shared international codes over national ones to guide ethical practice 2023 - Samochowiec et al. - Ethical dilemmas in contemporary psychiatry; Findings from a survey of National Psychiatric Associations in Europe
