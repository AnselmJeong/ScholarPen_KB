Why It Matters

  • Addresses systemic gaps in mental health care while balancing evidence-based treatment and human rights protections
  • Examines how policy frameworks influence coercive practices such as restraint and involuntary treatment
  • Informs ethical and legal standards for patient autonomy and safety in psychiatric settings
  • Highlights tensions between biomedical approaches and critiques of psychiatric power structures
