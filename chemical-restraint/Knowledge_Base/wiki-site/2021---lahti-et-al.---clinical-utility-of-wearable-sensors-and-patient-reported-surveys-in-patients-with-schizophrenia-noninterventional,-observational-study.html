Main Arguments & Findings

  • Core Claim: Wearable devices (Garmin vivofit, Philips Actiwatch) and smartphone-based PROs can be feasibly deployed in outpatient schizophrenia populations to collect longitudinal behavioral and self-report data, and machine learning models (elastic net) can derive predictive signals for clinical symptoms and functional outcomes.
